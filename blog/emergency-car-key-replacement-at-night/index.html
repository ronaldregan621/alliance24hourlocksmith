Safety Tips While You Wait

Being stranded at night in a parking lot or on a dark street is stressful. A few things that help:

  • Move to the most well-lit part of where you are. Near a business entrance, under a street light, or near other parked cars with people nearby.
  • Stay on the phone with a friend or family member while you wait if you feel unsafe.
  • Let the locksmith know exactly where you are when you call. Describe any landmarks so they find you quickly.
  • If the situation feels unsafe to stay and your car is legally parked, it is reasonable to leave and come back in the morning. Call us and we can schedule for when you return to the vehicle.
  • Do not accept help from strangers offering to open your car. Scam operators cruise areas looking for stranded drivers to approach directly.

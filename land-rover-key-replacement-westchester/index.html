Land Rover Key Types We Program

Older Flip Key Fob

Older Land Rover models through roughly 2011 including early Discovery and Freelander used a flip-style remote with a folding blade. These are simpler to replace than modern proximity keys.

Locksmith: $220 to $350 typical

Smart Proximity Key

Used on all Range Rover, Range Rover Sport, Discovery, Discovery Sport, and Defender from approximately 2012 onward. Stays in pocket or bag. Touch handle to unlock, push-button start. Requires JLR-compatible programming tools.

Locksmith: $350 to $600 typical

Defender Smart Key

Current Defender 90 and 110 (2020 onward) use an updated key with a similar proximity profile but more recent software stack. Programming confirmed on most VINs.

Locksmith: $375 to $600 typical

All-Keys-Lost Reset

Lost all keys on a Range Rover or Discovery? The vehicle's BCM must be reset and new keys paired from scratch. This is the most complex Land Rover job and takes the longest. Call with your VIN for a quote and we confirm whether it can be done on-site for your specific model year.

Land Rover and Range Rover Key Questions

How much does a Land Rover or Range Rover key replacement cost in Westchester?

With a specialist locksmith, most Land Rover and Range Rover key replacements run $350 to $600 for the key and programming combined. The dealer charges $500 to $800 or more, and some 2020-and-later Range Rover Sport and full-size Range Rover jobs have been reported at $1,500 or above. Call us with your year, model, and VIN for an exact quote.

Can you program Range Rover keys on-site, or do I need to go to the dealer?

For most models through the current generation of Discovery, Discovery Sport, Defender, and Range Rover Evoque, on-site programming is possible. Some of the most recent full-size Range Rover and Range Rover Sport models (2023 and newer) have proprietary SOTA (Software Over the Air) key pairing that can complicate third-party programming. Call us with your VIN and we confirm before you commit.

Which Land Rover models do you cover?

Range Rover full-size, Range Rover Sport, Range Rover Evoque, Range Rover Velar, Defender 90 and 110, Discovery, Discovery Sport, LR4, and older Freelander models. Call with your VIN to confirm, especially for 2022 and newer full-size Range Rover and Defender with newer software stacks.

Why are Land Rover keys so expensive compared to other makes?

Land Rover and Range Rover use PATS-encrypted smart keys that must be VIN-coded and paired via JLR-compatible diagnostic software. The keys themselves are more expensive to source than keys for mainstream makes, and the programming equipment is specialized. Even so, we are typically 30 to 50 percent less than the Westchester Land Rover dealers.

Are there Land Rover dealers in Westchester?

Yes. Land Rover White Plains, Land Rover New Rochelle, and Land Rover Mt. Kisco all serve Westchester. We are less expensive than all three for key replacement and we come to your location. No appointment, no drop-off.

# Alliance 24hr Locksmith

Alliance 24hr Locksmith is a licensed and insured mobile locksmith service operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week across the greater New York metropolitan area, including Westchester County NY, Fairfield County CT, Nassau County (Long Island) NY, and northern New Jersey.

## Services

- Emergency lockouts: residential, car, and commercial
- Car lockout and on-site vehicle entry
- House and apartment lockout
- Car key replacement and programming (transponder keys, key fobs, proximity keys)
- Lock rekey (change the lock without replacing it)
- Key duplication
- Key fob and RFID access card programming and duplication
- Commercial emergency lockouts
- High security lock installation (Medeco, Mul-T-Lock)
- Smart lock installation
- Master key system design and installation
- Access control systems

Audi Key Types We Program

Flip Remote Key

Used on most Audi models through 2010 including B6/B7 A4, early Q7, A3, and TT. The metal key blade folds into the remote housing. Distinctive Audi-style key that many owners still rely on today.

Locksmith: $220 to $320 typical

Standard Remote Key

Non-flip remote used on B8 and B8.5 A4 (2009 to 2016), A6 C7, early Q5, and similar. Separate metal blade and a larger remote head with lock, unlock, and trunk buttons.

Locksmith: $280 to $380 typical

Advance Key

Audi's proximity keyless entry system found on Q5, Q7, Q8, A4 B9, A6 C8 and later models. Touch the door handle to unlock without removing the fob from your pocket. VIN coding required for pairing.

Locksmith: $350 to $500 typical

All-Keys-Lost Reset

If every Audi key has been lost, the immobilizer must be cleared via ODIS-compatible tools and a fresh key coded to the VIN. Possible on-site for most models. Call with your VIN for a quote.

Audi Key Questions

How much does an Audi key replacement cost in Westchester?

A4 and A5 keys typically run $300 to $400 with us. Q5 and Q7 Advance Keys run $350 to $500. Dealer pricing for these same keys is $400 to $850 depending on model, and Audi dealers charge a separate diagnostic or programming fee on top. Call us with your year and model for an exact quote.

What is the Audi Advance Key and can you program it?

Advance Key is Audi's name for its proximity keyless entry system, available on A4, A5, A6, Q5, Q7, Q8 and other models from around 2011 onward. The key stays in your pocket or bag. Touching the door handle unlocks the car, and the start button works without removing the fob. Yes, we program Advance Key fobs on-site.

My Audi has the older flip key. Can you replace that?

Yes. Audi flip remotes were standard on many models through 2010 or so, including the B6 and B7 A4, early Q7, and TT. The metal blade folds into the remote body. We cut and program replacement flip keys for most of these models.

Do you need the VIN for Audi key programming?

Yes. Audi uses VIN-based coding to pair a new key to the vehicle's immobilizer. We will ask for the VIN when you call. You can usually find it on the lower driver-side windshield corner or on the registration card in the glove box.

Can you add a spare Audi key if I only have one?

Yes, and we strongly recommend it. Adding a spare while you still have a working key is much faster and less expensive than a lost-all-keys reset. Spare key programming for most Audi models takes 30 to 45 minutes on-site.

Volvo Key Types We Program

Older Remote Key

Older Volvo models through approximately 2007, including the S40, S60 first generation, V70, and XC70, used a standard flip-style or standard remote key with a transponder chip. More straightforward to replace than modern proximity fobs.

Locksmith: $180 to $280 typical

Sensus Proximity Fob

Standard key fob for XC90, XC60, XC40, S60, V60, and V90 models from 2015 onward. Proximity entry, push-button start. Volvo VIDA-compatible programming required to pair the fob to the car's CEM (Central Electronic Module).

Locksmith: $280 to $450 typical

Digital Key (App-Based)

Newer Volvo models support the Volvo Cars app as a digital key via Bluetooth. If the app loses connection or the phone is lost, the physical fob remains the primary backup. We focus on the physical fob replacement.

Locksmith: App-based, see fob pricing

All-Keys-Lost Reset

If all Volvo keys are lost, the CEM must be reprogrammed with new key credentials. One of the more involved jobs for European luxury makes. Call with your VIN and we confirm feasibility and quote.

Volvo Key Questions

How much does Volvo key replacement cost in Westchester?

A Sensus proximity fob replacement with programming runs $280 to $450 with us. The Volvo dealer in the White Plains area typically charges $400 to $650 for the same key. Older remote key replacement runs $180 to $280. Call with your year and model for an exact quote.

Can you program a Volvo proximity fob on-site?

Yes. Volvo proximity fobs for XC90, XC60, XC40, S60, and V60 require Volvo VIDA-compatible diagnostic tools to pair with the car's Central Electronic Module. We carry that equipment and come to your location anywhere in Westchester.

How long does Volvo key programming take?

Most Sensus fob programming takes 35 to 55 minutes at your location. All-keys-lost situations take longer. We give you a realistic time window on the call.

What is the CEM and why does it matter for key programming?

The CEM is Volvo's Central Electronic Module, the control unit that manages door locks, keyless entry, and immobilizer functions. When a new fob is programmed, it is paired to the CEM via VIN-specific credentials. This is why Volvo key replacement requires professional tools rather than a simple self-programming procedure.
